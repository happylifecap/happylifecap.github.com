About Us

We invest in Technology, Healthcare, and Energy.

Our strategic areas of interest are AI, IT, Analytics, Integrated Networking, and Big Data.

Our focus is on Data Management and Interpretation, Intelligent Software Platforms, Interactive and Ambulatory Robotics, Advanced Personalized Healthcare, and Efficient, Sustainable Energy.

We look for large market opportunities with a clear opportunity for traction and exit.

Tens of global experienced resident CEOs to help entrepreneurs to manage and to grow.
